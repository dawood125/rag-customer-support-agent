NeuralDesk - AI Customer Support Platform

What is NeuralDesk?
NeuralDesk is an AI-powered customer support SaaS platform that uses RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) to answer customer questions based on a company's knowledge base.

Key Features:
1. Multi-tenant architecture - Each company has isolated data
2. RAG pipeline - AI answers from YOUR documents, not generic internet
3. WhatsApp integration - Reply to customers on their favorite channel
4. Real-time chat - Instant responses with live typing indicators
5. Document upload - Support for PDF, Word, and text files

How does RAG work?
RAG stands for Retrieval Augmented Generation. When a customer asks a question:
- The system searches the company's uploaded documents for relevant information
- The most relevant chunks are passed to the AI
- The AI generates a contextual answer based on those chunks
- This ensures answers are accurate and based on real company data

Pricing Plans:
- Free: 1 document, 100 messages per month
- Basic: 10 documents, 1000 messages per month
- Premium: Unlimited documents and messages
